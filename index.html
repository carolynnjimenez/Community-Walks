Glen Park

I have called Glen Park my home for the last year. For those interested in visiting my neighborhood, I chose a few highlights:

Glen Park BART Station: featured in "The Pursuit of Happyness" iconic Brutalist architecture

Glen Canyon Park: great recreation area and a short hike to Twin Peaks

Bird and Beckett Books: live music and poetry readings that are currently being streamed through social media. I loved getting lost in these stacks on a Saturday afternoon.

Cup Coffeeshop: Glen Park boasts many neighborhood coffee shops, but Cup is by far my favorite (and the closest to my home).
